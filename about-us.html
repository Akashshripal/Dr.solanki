Chairman's Message

We are an outcome of 35 years of passion, experience, and skills backed by dedication delivering.... Simply Eye Care.

Dear Friends,

Welcome to Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital.

For more than three decades Dr. Solanki Eye Hospital (DSEH) has been serving people from all walks of life with exceptional eye care. From a humble Cataract, we have grown to a hospital serving people not only from the city of Bengaluru but across the country and beyond the shores.

With eminent Ophthalmologists, we are committed to delivering world-class eye care services to an increasing number of people who have come to consider DSEH as their preferred choice of the eye hospital. Our outstanding team of paramedical and support staff strives to ensure the best possible treatment outcomes.

We believe in and practice the highest levels of medical ethics. Patient safety and infection control assume the highest priority at DSEH. We recognize that technology plays a vital role in delivering quality eye care services and hence invest consistently in procuring the best of equipment to serve our patients.

Our doctors take time to carefully assess and diagnose the issues you are having with your eyes and prescribe the best course of action for you as a patient, to have the best possible quality of life.

We are an outcome of 35 years of passion, experience, and skills backed by dedication delivering….Simply Eye Care.

With Warm Regards,

Dr. Narpat Solanki, Chief Medical Director
